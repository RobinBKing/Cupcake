RECIPES

CHOCOLATE

  1. 2 cups coco
  2. 1 cups sugar
  3. 2 cups flour
  4. 1 tsp b.soda
  5. 1 tbsp b.powder
  6. 1 tsp vanilla
  7. 3 eggs

Directions

Sift flour, blend eggs, whisk all ingredients. Pour into cupcake pan and bake at 350 for 25 minutes. Cool and frost.
